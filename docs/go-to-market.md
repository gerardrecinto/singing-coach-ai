# Go-To-Market

## Positioning

Singing Coach AI gives singers actionable feedback from a recording. It combines audio analysis with AI coaching so users get concrete practice guidance instead of raw pitch charts.

## Best First Customer

- Beginner and intermediate singers practicing alone.
- Vocal coaches who want lightweight pre-lesson analysis.
- Creators preparing covers, auditions, or live performances.

## Monetization Path

| Tier | Offer | Buyer |
|---|---|---|
| CLI / Open Source | Local analysis and raw metrics. | Developers and music technologists. |
| Web App | Upload limits, saved sessions, coaching history, practice plans. | Individual singers. |
| Coach Dashboard | Student uploads, weekly reports, assignment tracking. | Vocal coaches and music schools. |
| API | Audio-analysis endpoint and coaching JSON. | Music apps and creator tools. |

## Launch Hooks

1. "Stop guessing why your take sounds off."
2. "Upload a recording and get one practice plan, not twenty confusing charts."
3. "Pitch, rhythm, dynamics, and tone feedback in one pass."
